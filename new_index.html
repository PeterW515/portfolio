My Background

Ater graduating from Clemson University in 2018, I began working for Manhattan Associates, a supply chain software company. As a consultant and then business analyst, I worked on the functional side of the software. Around the middle of 2020, I began to think I wanted to move in to more technical work.

The Spark

I thought a good first step (and trial run) into programming would be to learn Java and take Oracle's certification exam. I passed and became an Oracle Certified Associate, Java SE 8 Programmer.

My Development Experience

fter the Java certification, I enrolled in the Georgia Tech Full Stack Coding Bootcamp in the beginning of 2021. This rigorous program teaches the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Javascript and then quickly accelerates into API development, Node.js, OOP basics, servers, Express, SQL, MVC, Progressive Web Apps, React, and many other frameworks, libraries, and packages.

Future Goals

I've developed this portfolio with links to some of my projects, as well as my Github account. I hope to use this to showcase my work and launch my career as a full stack developer!
